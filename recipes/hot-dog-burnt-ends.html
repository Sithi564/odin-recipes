Hot dog burnt ends

The tastiest dish on the menu

Ingredients

  • 2 packages beef hot dog
  • 1 cup yellow mustard
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine hot dogs, mustard, brown sugar, cayenne pepper, onion powder, and black pepper in a large resealable bag. Shake bag so hot dogs are well coated with mustard and spice mixture. Spread out seasoned hot dogs onto the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove hot dogs and cut them into thirds. Increase oven temperature to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  5. Step 5

    6. Combine hot dog pieces and BBQ sauce in a bowl and mix until hot dogs are completely coated with sauce. Return to the baking sheet.

  6. Step 6

    7. Bake in the preheated oven for an additional 10 minutes.
